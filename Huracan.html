The Lamborghini Huracan is a spots car manufactured by Italian automotive manufacturer Lamborghini.Replacing the previous V10 from the Gallardo, the Huracan made its worldwide debut at the 2014 Geneva Auto Show, and was released in the market in the second quarter of 2014.

Specifications:

1.Engine type:V-10

2.Displacement:5.2 L/318

3.Fuel System:Gasoline Direct Injection

4.Maximum Horsepower/RPM:630 @ 8000

5.Maximum Torque/RPM:442 @ 6500
